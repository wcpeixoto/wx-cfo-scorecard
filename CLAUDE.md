## Communication style
- Use plain language. Avoid jargon; if a technical term is unavoidable, define it in one short clause.
- Be succinct. Lead with the answer, then only the detail needed to act.
- Prefer short paragraphs and tight bullets over long explanations.
- Skip preamble, restatement, and filler.
